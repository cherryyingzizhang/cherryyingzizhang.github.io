Hi, welcome to my website! My name is Cherry, and I'm a self-motivated engineer, driven by my curiosity and passion.

Right now I study Mechatronics Engineering (Honours, Co-op) at the University of Waterloo and am always excited about learning new technology and solving challenging problems. I love working on projects that make a positive change and improve the wellbeing of people, and my career goal matches that.

I've worked with children, tutored students, and volunteered at events where I influence and inspire people to learn about many different exciting fields. I've taught music to refugee children, tutored for high school subjects, volunteered at hackathon workshops in Japan, and volunteered at events where I can inspire people to do engineering.

I'm currently open minded to learn a ton of amazing things that come my way during my university life. I've met so many inspirational people who motivated me to try even harder (e.g. I want to try and self-learn machine learning), and I've experienced and learned lots of things from my past internships (which are mostly Android software engineering internships, including at Microsoft).

In terms of hobbies, I have a passion for music which has led me to participate in a variety of ensembles over the years. I like to sing, and have been formally playing the violin (and recently the viola) since I was a kid. I also really like history trivia, philosophy, and have been interested in the study of psychology.
